OUR STORY

Crafting elegance since 1922

HERITAGE

Founded in London's Savile Row in 1922, KINGSMAN began as a small tailoring house dedicated to crafting exceptional garments for gentlemen of distinction.

Through decades of sartorial evolution, we've maintained our commitment to traditional craftsmanship while innovating to meet the needs of the modern gentleman. Each generation of master tailors has added to our legacy, creating a living tradition of excellence.

Today, our atelier combines century-old techniques with contemporary design, ensuring every piece honors our heritage while exceeding modern expectations.

THE KINGSMAN CRAFT

Precision Tailoring

Each pattern is hand-drafted to individual measurements, with precision down to the millimeter. Our 25-point fitting process ensures perfect proportions.

Exceptional Materials

We source only the world's finest fabrics - from rare English wools to luxurious Italian silks - each selected for its unique qualities and durability.

Hand Finishing

Every buttonhole is stitched by hand, every seam allowance carefully finished. Our tailors spend over 50 hours on each suit's handwork alone.

Bespoke Consultations

Schedule a private appointment with our master tailors for a completely custom garment.

Personal Styling

Our style advisors will help you build a cohesive wardrobe tailored to your lifestyle.

In-Store Alterations

Complimentary basic alterations on all purchases with our expert tailors.
