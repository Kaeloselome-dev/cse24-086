Our Services

Bespoke Tailoring

Experience the pinnacle of sartorial excellence with our fully bespoke service. Each garment is crafted to your exact measurements and personal style preferences.

Made-to-Measure

Our made-to-measure service offers personalized fits with select fabric options, providing a perfect balance between customization and convenience.

Alterations

Professional alterations ensure your Kingsman garments fit perfectly. We offer complimentary basic alterations on all full-price purchases.

Wardrobe Consultation

Our style advisors will help you build a cohesive wardrobe that reflects your personal style and meets your lifestyle needs.

Fabric Library

Access our exclusive collection of the world's finest fabrics from renowned mills in England, Italy, and Scotland.

Leather Care

Specialized cleaning and conditioning services to maintain the beauty and longevity of your Kingsman leather goods.

The Bespoke Process

1

Initial Consultation

A 90-minute session with our master tailor to discuss your needs, select fabrics, and take precise measurements.

2

First Fitting

Try your garment in the "baste" stage where adjustments are marked directly on the fabric by our tailors.

3

Final Fitting

Experience your nearly completed garment for final refinements before the finishing touches are applied.
