FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We offer a 30-day return policy for all regular purchases. Items must be in original condition with all tags attached. Bespoke items are final sale. Returns are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your package.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website. For international shipments, tracking may take 24-48 hours to update after dispatch.

Yes, we offer complimentary basic alterations on all suits purchased directly from us within the first 30 days. For more complex alterations or after this period, our expert tailors can provide services for a reasonable fee. Contact your nearest store for details.

We recommend dry cleaning only when necessary (2-3 times per year). Between cleanings, brush with a clothes brush and steam to refresh. Always use a wide, padded hanger to maintain shape. Store in a breathable garment bag when not in use.

Each KINGSMAN suit represents over 120 hours of craftsmanship using techniques perfected over a century. We use only the finest fabrics from renowned mills, hand-cut patterns for optimal drape, and employ traditional construction methods that allow the garment to mold to your body over time.
